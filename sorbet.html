Sorbet Fraise

This a short description of the amazing sorbet fraise (looks like an ice cream but it's better for your health).

Ingredients

  • Fruit (fresh of frozen)
  • Water
  • Some type of cream
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Put fruits onto magnifient machine to make sorbet
  2. Add sugar
  3. Add cream
  4. Eat before it melts
