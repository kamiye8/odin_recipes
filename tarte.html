Tarte

This is a supposed to be a description of the fantastic recipe called "tarte aux poireaux".
If you never had one in your life, don't worry, you can still make one

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • puff pastry
  • half a red onion
  • 3 or 4 leeks
  • grated cheese

Steps

  1. Chop chop leeks and onion
  2. Crack eggs into bol
  3. Mix all together
  4. Put puff pastry onto oven plate
  5. VOILA (almost)
